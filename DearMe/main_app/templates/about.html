About DearMe

Time moves forward, and we cannot go back. But memories and messages can travel to the future. DearMe is a bridge between today and tomorrow, letting you send letters and share moments with your future self or loved ones.

A note of encouragement, a heartfelt message, or a memory to revisit — DearMe keeps your emotions alive, reaching across time to touch hearts, including your own in the years to come.

Use the Sad Button for comfort, write daily diary entries, or create memories to open later. Each message is a whisper of love, hope, and connection, waiting for the right moment.

Even across the distance of years, you are never truly alone. Your feelings, thoughts, and love endure, reaching forward to bring comfort, joy, and reflection.

— DearMe @ 2025
